Automating Brain Tumor Detection

Leveraging state-of-the-art Deep Learning for early and accurate diagnosis.

Objective

This project aims to assist radiologists by automating brain tumor detection from MRI scans, improving both diagnostic speed and accuracy.

Technologies Used

CLAHE for contrast enhancement, RGB Fusion for color balancing, TransUNet for segmentation, and ResNet for classification. Grad-CAM and LIME provide transparency and explainability.
